ℹ ABOUT

What is Resume Kombat AI?

An AI-powered resume comparison and hiring intelligence platform that turns dry candidate evaluation into an immersive, explainable, esports-style experience.

OUR MISSION

Hiring decisions matter. We make the analysis transparent, fast, and even entertaining.

Traditional resume screening is slow, subjective, and opaque. Recruiters spend an average of 7 seconds on a resume before deciding. Resume Kombat AI uses Claude AI to do what humans can't — evaluate both candidates deeply across every dimension simultaneously, generate explainable scores with full reasoning, and present the results in a format that actually engages hiring teams.

Every score has reasoning. No black-box outputs. No "candidate A is better" without showing exactly why. The battle format isn't just fun — it forces AI to reason category by category, making the output more thorough and auditable.

THE PROCESS

How It Works

01 — PARSE
Resume Parsing
PDF/DOCX/TXT files are read client-side. Text is extracted and structured: name, skills, experience, education, projects, certifications, achievements, links.
02 — ANALYZE
AI Analysis
Claude AI receives structured resume content and evaluates both candidates across 6 weighted categories with full contextual reasoning per round.
03 — BATTLE
Battle Engine
Scores feed the animated battle arena. Health bars drain in real time. Special attack combos trigger when elite skill stacks are detected.
04 — INSIGHT
Recruiter Intel
Full dashboard: radar charts, ATS scores, skill gap analysis, JD matching, hire/no-hire recommendation, and personalized improvement plans.
SCORING METHODOLOGY

The Weighted Battle System

Scoring weights mirror real-world recruiter priorities for tech roles. Each category is scored 0–100 with AI-generated reasoning before a weighted total is calculated.

R3Project Warfare20%Portfolio quality, real-world usage, open source, complexity

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data stored or used for training?▶
No. Resume text is processed entirely in your browser and sent only to the Anthropic Claude API for analysis. We don't store resume data on any server. Anthropic's API usage policies apply for the AI inference portion.
What file formats are supported?▶
Currently TXT files are fully supported in the browser-only version. The planned backend version will support PDF (via pdfplumber), DOCX (via python-docx), and plain text. For best results with the current version, copy-paste your resume content into a .txt file.
How accurate is the AI scoring?▶
The AI provides relative scoring based on resume content — not absolute truth. It's a decision-support tool, not a replacement for human judgment. Scores reflect what's written in the resume; if a candidate hasn't listed achievements clearly, they'll score lower even if they're excellent in reality. Always use AI output as one signal among many.
Can I use this for actual hiring decisions?▶
Resume Kombat AI is designed to assist and accelerate human hiring decisions, not replace them. Use it to shortlist candidates, identify skill gaps, and structure your evaluation — but always conduct proper interviews and reference checks before making offers.
Does it detect bias or discriminatory patterns?▶
The system scores purely on professional content: skills, experience, projects, education, certifications, and ATS quality. It does not consider name, gender, ethnicity, or personal information. However, all AI systems can have emergent biases — use outputs critically and in combination with diverse human review.
How does Tournament Mode work?▶
Tournament Mode runs a knockout bracket for 4, 8, or 16 resumes. AI evaluates each matchup independently and advances winners round by round until a champion is crowned. It's ideal for comparing large candidate pools from a hiring campaign.
What is JD Matching Mode?▶
When you paste a job description, the AI adds keyword matching and semantic alignment to the analysis. Each candidate receives a JD match percentage alongside their standard battle scores, and the recruiter recommendation is tailored to the specific role requirements.
Is Resume Kombat AI free?▶
The current version is a portfolio/demo project. API costs are covered by the Anthropic API key configured at deployment time. A production SaaS version with usage tiers is planned.
